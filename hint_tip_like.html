Background color matters

Hover your mouse in and out of the box (or touch screeen) and see it increase and reduce the glare. Often dyslexics find yellow a hard color to look at.

Blue has less glare.

Solution

  • Print on blue paper
  • Wear blue glasses when reading on white paper
  • Put blue filter over paper

Difficulty reading

There are lots of visual effects that dyslexics have.

Everyone mixes things up

'What I if told you........' you read that sentence wrong

Solution

  • Read twice- once to get the words and once to get the meaning
  • Start reading everything you see - then you are ahead if asked to read it

Short term memory

Very difficult to remember:

  • lists
  • digits in numbers
  • spellings
  • directions
  • instructions
  • to finish sentences
  • time relationships
  • simple procedures
  • Handwriting letter formation

>

Solution

  • Photograph it
  • Print screen it

Confuse left and right

Have no idea that there is a difference between left and right

Solution

Back of left hand give L between first finger and thumb back of right hand doesn't
